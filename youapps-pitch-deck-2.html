Why We Exist: A Personal Mission

As a legally blind developer, I've spent my life struggling with technology that wasn't designed for people like me.

Every day, I face the same challenges that 2.2 billion people with disabilities encounter - tiny keyboards, inaccessible interfaces, and apps that prioritize aesthetics over usability.

YouApps exists because I refuse to accept that technology should exclude anyone.
