Smart Alert System

Alerts adapt to obstacle distance and your movement speed

< 30cm - CRITICAL
Continuous alarm + rapid beeps. Stop immediately.
30-50cm - Very Close
10 beeps per second. Obstacle imminent.
50-80cm - Close
5 beeps per second. Caution needed.
80-150cm - Medium
2-3 beeps per second. Obstacle detected.
150-300cm - Far
Slow beeps. Awareness alert.
> 300cm - Clear
No alerts. Path is clear.
