iOS Permissions Explained

Full Access Permission

iOS shows a scary warning when you enable "Full Access" for keyboards. Here's what it actually means for Big Keys:

  • What iOS Says: The keyboard could access everything you type
  • What Big Keys Does: We use this permission only for features like sound effects and haptic feedback
  • What We Don't Do: We don't read, store, or transmit your keystrokes anywhere
