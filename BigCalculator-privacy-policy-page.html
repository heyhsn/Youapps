Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Big Calculator free?

We believe everyone deserves access to accessible tools. Big Calculator is our contribution to making technology more inclusive for people with vision difficulties or anyone who prefers large, clear displays.

Do you really collect zero data?

Yes, absolutely zero. We don't even know you're using the app. There are no analytics, no crash reporting, no usage statistics - nothing. The app works 100% offline on your device.

What about the currency converter?

Exchange rates are pre-loaded in the app and work offline. While rates may not be real-time, this ensures your privacy is never compromised for a feature.

Will you ever add ads or paid features?

No. Big Calculator will always be free, ad-free, and without any paid features or subscriptions.

How do you sustain the app without revenue?

Big Calculator is part of our Big Apps suite of accessibility-focused applications. It's our way of giving back to the community.
